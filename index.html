Lara Croft

The Iconic Archaeologist and Adventurer

Biography

Lara Croft is a famous archaeologist and adventurer known for her exciting quests and treasure hunts. She is the main character in the Tomb Raider game series.

Fun Facts

  • Lara Croft first appeared in 1996.
  • She is one of the most well-known female characters in gaming.
  • Lara is considered a feminist icon in the gaming industry.
